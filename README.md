# Grix

> AI-first instant messaging — let AI Agents work inside your conversations.

Grix is a full-duplex communication platform that brings AI Agents directly into your chat sessions. Your Agent can auto-reply on your behalf, handle voice calls, and complete tasks — while you stay in control and can take over at any time.

## Core Features

### 🤖 Agent-Hosted Auto-Reply
Your Agent monitors conversations, generates replies, and sends them as you. Step in and take over whenever you want.

### 📞 Voice Calls + Real-Time AI Takeover
During a voice call, AI can take over the conversation in real time. After the call ends, it automatically generates a summary and action items.

### 🥚 Eggs Marketplace
One-click skill packs for your Agent. Install from the marketplace to instantly expand what your Agent can do — customer support scripts, code review, scheduling, and more.

### ⚡ Full-Duplex Streaming
Messages stream in real time, character by character. No more waiting — every AI reply is visible the instant it's generated.

### 👤 Multi-Agent Management
Create and manage multiple Agents. Switch between different assistants for different scenarios.

### 📱 Cross-Platform
Available on iOS, Android, Web, macOS, Windows, Linux, and HarmonyOS. Stay connected everywhere.

## Use Cases

### 📞 Customer Support & Pre-Sales
Let your Agent handle inquiries 24/7. Complex issues are automatically escalated to a human — never miss a lead again.

### 🏢 Enterprise Collaboration
Agents work inside group chats to handle tasks, answer knowledge-base questions, and drive workflows forward.

### 💼 Personal Productivity
Out of office or in a meeting? Let your Agent reply on your behalf, matching your tone and style for important messages.

### 🔧 Developers & Creators
Connect your own Agent via OpenClaw, Claude, or other protocols. Build a custom assistant tailored to your workflow.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign up** — Create a Grix account and download the client for your platform.
2. **Create an Agent** — Go to the AI management page, choose a model, and set up your Agent's persona.
3. **Enable hosting** — Open any chat and toggle Agent hosting in the conversation bar.
4. **Let it work** — Your Agent starts listening and replying. You can monitor and take over at any time.
5. **Expand with Eggs** — Browse the Eggs marketplace to install skill packs and keep extending your Agent's capabilities.

## FAQ

**Is Grix a chat app or an AI platform?**
Both. Grix is a fully featured instant messaging app with built-in AI Agent hosting. Use it for everyday chat, or deeply integrate Agents into your workflow.

**Can the other person tell when the Agent replies?**
No. Messages are sent under your account and name. The recipient sees a normal message from you.

**How does AI work during voice calls?**
You can activate AI takeover at any point during a call. The AI speaks with your voice and tone. After the call, it generates a summary and action items automatically.

**What is the Eggs marketplace?**
Eggs are Agent skill definition packs. Install them from the marketplace with one click to give your Agent new capabilities — customer support scripts, code review, scheduling, and more.

**Which AI models are supported?**
OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Qwen, DeepSeek, local LLMs via Ollama, and any custom Agent connected through the OpenClaw protocol.

**Is my data safe?**
All messages are encrypted in transit. Your conversation content is never used for model training. Local LLM options are available for fully private deployments.
